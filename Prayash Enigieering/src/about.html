Who We Are

Founded in 2021, Prayash Engineering is a dynamic engineering services and manufacturing company based in Halol, Gujarat. Led by Mr. Nilesh Patel, who brings over 18 years of multidisciplinary engineering experience, the company has rapidly grown into a reliable partner for complex industrial requirements.

We specialize in mechanical fabrication, material handling equipment, industrial pipelines, hydraulic systems, and precision-machined components. Our approach combines technical knowledge, quality assurance, and a strong commitment to customer satisfaction.

Certified under ISO 9001:2015, Prayash Engineering ensures consistent quality, safety, and performance across every project — from concept design to final commissioning.

"Quality isn't an act, it is a habit. At Prayash, we ensure every weld, every cut, and every assembly meets the highest industrial standards."
