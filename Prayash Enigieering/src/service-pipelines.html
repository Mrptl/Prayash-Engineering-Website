Industrial Pipeline Projects

Expert turnkey execution of utility and process piping networks.

Flowing with Precision

Industrial pipelines are critical infrastructure for power plants, chemical units, and pharmaceutical facilities. Prayash Engineering undertakes turnkey piping projects, ensuring seamless flow and distribution of utilities like water, air, steam, and specialized process fluids.

Our expertise covers both Carbon Steel (CS) utility lines and high-purity Stainless Steel (SS) piping for hygienic applications. We manage the entire lifecycle from layout design and isometric drawing to fabrication, erection, non-destructive testing, and final hydrostatic pressure testing.

Pipeline Services:

  • Utility Piping: Compressed air, cooling water, and chiller lines for manufacturing plants.
  • Pharma & Food Grade: SS 304/316L piping with orbital welding for high sanitary standards.
  • Fire Hydrant Lines: Design and installation of fire safety piping networks.
  • Steam & Condensate: Efficient IBR and non-IBR steam piping execution.
  • Testing & Commissioning: Hydro-testing and radiography to ensure joint integrity.
