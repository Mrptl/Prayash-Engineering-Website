EOT Crane Maintenance & AMC

Maximizing machine uptime with expert preventive maintenance and rapid breakdown support.

Comprehensive Crane Care

Cranes are the lifeline of industrial operations. A sudden breakdown can halt production and lead to significant losses. At Prayash Engineering, we offer specialized maintenance services designed to keep your material handling equipment running at peak efficiency.

We provide flexible Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) tailored to your operational schedule. Our expert technicians perform rigorous safety audits, mechanical adjustments, and electrical troubleshooting to prevent failures before they occur.

Our Service Scope:

  • Preventive Maintenance: Scheduled inspections of wire ropes, brakes, wheels, and limit switches.
  • Breakdown Support: 24/7 emergency repair services to restore operations quickly.
  • Busbar (DSL) Systems: Installation and replacement of shrouded bus bar systems for safe power transmission.
  • Testing & Certification: Third-party load testing and certification services for compliance.
