Heavy Precision Fabrication

Building the backbone of industry with structural steel excellence.

Structural Excellence

Prayash Engineering brings specialized expertise in heavy fabrication. Whether it's structural framework for a new plant, support structures for massive machinery, or industrial storage tanks, we deliver precision and durability.

Our facility is equipped for cutting, bending, and welding heavy steel sections. We adhere to strict welding standards (WPS/PQR) and ensure every joint meets the required strength specifications through non-destructive testing (NDT) where applicable.

We handle projects ranging from pre-engineered buildings (PEB) components to complex custom skids for process equipment.

Fabrication Services:

  • Industrial Sheds: Roof trusses, columns, and gantry girders.
  • Tanks & Vessels: SS and MS storage tanks for chemicals, water, or oil.
  • Machinery Skids: Robust base frames for pumps, compressors, and generators.
  • Platforms & Walkways: Access ladders, handrails, and grating platforms emphasizing safety.
