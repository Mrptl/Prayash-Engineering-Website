Hydraulic Hose Pipes & Fittings

Precision-engineered hydraulic connectors for leak-free power transmission.

Fluid Power Solutions

Hydraulic systems rely on integrity. Even a minor leak can lead to pressure loss, contamination, and system failure. Prayash Engineering provides high-quality hydraulic hose assemblies and fittings designed to withstand high pressure, vibration, and harsh industrial environments.

We stock a wide range of hoses and end fittings, allowing us to fabricate custom assemblies on-demand. Our crimping technology ensures a secure grip, and every assembly is visually inspected and pressure-tested as required.

Capabilities:

  • Hose Assembly: Custom length hydraulic hoses with BSP, JIC, ORFS, or Metric fittings.
  • Material Variety: Wire braided hoses (R1, R2, 4SP, 4SH) and thermoplastic hoses.
  • Adapters & QRCs: Comprehensive range of adapters and Quick Release Couplings for easy connectivity.
  • On-Site Service: Hose replacement and system troubleshooting at your facility.
