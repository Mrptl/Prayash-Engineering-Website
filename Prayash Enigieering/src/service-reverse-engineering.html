Reverse Engineering

Breathing new life into old machinery. Import substitution and spare part development.

Re-Engineer. Improve. Manufacture.

Industrial machinery is built to last, but specific parts wear out. When OEM spares are obsolete, expensive, or have long lead times, reverse engineering is the solution. Prayash Engineering specializes in analyzing worn-out or broken components and recreating them with original—or better—specifications.

Our process involves detailed dimensional measurement, material chemical analysis (spectroscopy), and creating 2D/3D manufacturing drawings. This allows us to manufacture precision spares for textile, plastic, and heavy engineering industries as direct import substitutes.

Our Process:

  • 1. Analysis: Physical inspection and material testing of the sample part.
  • 2. Digitization: Drafting precise CAD models and engineering drawings.
  • 3. Optimization: Suggesting material upgrades or design tweaks for better longevity.
  • 4. Manufacturing: CNC machining and finishing to exact tolerances.
