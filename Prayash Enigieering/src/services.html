Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our engineering services

What types of cranes does Prayash Engineering service?
We service and supply spares for EOT (Electric Overhead Travel) cranes, Gantry cranes, Monorails, and specialized lifting equipment. Our services include installation, preventive maintenance, breakdown support, load testing, and complete AMC packages.
Do you provide hydraulic hose assembly services for industrial equipment?
Yes, we manufacture and supply hydraulic hose assemblies, fittings, and complete hydraulic system solutions. Our products are pressure-tested and comply with industry safety standards.
Can you fabricate custom lifting equipment for wind turbine installations?
Yes, we specialize in designing and fabricating windmill tower lifting hooks, spreader beams, and custom lifting fixtures. These are engineered for heavy loads with appropriate safety factors.
What industries do you serve with your pipeline services?
We serve pharmaceutical (with sanitary SS 304/316L piping), chemical processing, power plants, utilities, and general industrial sectors. We provide complete piping solutions from design to commissioning with proper documentation.
Do you offer reverse engineering services for obsolete parts?
Yes, we provide reverse engineering services for obsolete or hard-to-find machinery parts. This includes dimensional analysis, material identification, and manufacturing to original specifications, enabling import substitution.
