Crane Spares & Lifting Solutions

Engineered for safety, designed for strength. Complete lifting solutions for industrial applications.

Reliable Lifting Equipment

Prayash Engineering specializes in the design, manufacturing, and supply of heavy-duty lifting equipment and crane spares. We understand that material handling is the backbone of any manufacturing facility, and downtime is not an option.

Our solutions range from critical spares for EOT cranes to custom-fabricated lifting accessories like spreader beams and windmill tower hooks. All our products undergo rigorous quality checks to ensure they meet international safety standards.

Our Offerings Include:

  • Crane Spares: Long-travel wheels, gearboxes, wire rope drums, brakes, and electrical panels.
  • Windmill Tools: Specialized tower lifting hooks, anchor cages, and blade lifting fixtures.
  • Custom Fixtures: Spreader beams for uneven loads, C-hooks for coils, and plate lifting clamps.
  • Modernization: Upgrading existing manual cranes to motorized systems.
